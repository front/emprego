About Frontkom

Frontkom is a digital consultancy agency based in Norway that specializes in developing and implementing open-source solutions.

Frontkom has an expanding team and is recruiting a full-time Trainee Ruby on Rails Developer to work at the Funchal office, or remotely from Portugal.

Please note that, as this is a Trainee entry-level position, no in-depth knowledge of the technologies described below is required. Frontkom will provide time and resources for training during the onboarding of the developer.

The selected candidate will have primary responsibilities to learn and implement web solutions using Ruby on Rails framework.

Profile

  • Ease of communication (spoken and written).
  • Ability to interact with customers, technical and non-technical
  • Strong sense of responsibility and teamwork.
  • Good organization and time management skills.
  • Interest in learning new technologies.
  • Availability to travel abroad.

Skills

Requirements

  • Minimum educational qualifications: High-school (12th year).
  • Good knowledge of English (spoken and written).
  • Development experience with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
  • Familiarity with MVC and RESTful.
  • Knowledge of relational databases (SQL).

Valued skills

  • Higher education in Computer Engineering or equivalent.
  • Familiarity with testing tools.
  • Knowledge of Ruby language, Ruby on Rails framework and libraries like RSpec
  • Knowledge of version control systems (ex: Git).
  • Knowledge with Project Management platforms, such as Jira.
